Welcome

At Diamond Health Club, you can stay healthy year-round and have fun doing it! We offer something for everyone. Our state-of-the-art facilities can challenge the most seasoned athlete, while remaining friendly to our newest members. We have classes for everyone—from children and teens to adults and seniors. No matter who you are, DHC offers a class for you.

DHC also provides several membership options. You can register as an individual or a family. We also provide special couples plans. Planning to visit Seattle a few days, weeks, or a month? Our great temporary memberships can be tailored to the needs of any visitor. Temporary memberships also make great Christmas gifts.

Facilities

  • 2 weight rooms
  • Olympic pool with at least 3 lanes always open
  • Warm, 4-foot deep therapeutic pool
  • 2 gymnasiums with full size basketball courts
  • Exercise room for our extensive free and class instruction
  • Climbing wall
  • 3 racquetball courts
  • On-site child care
